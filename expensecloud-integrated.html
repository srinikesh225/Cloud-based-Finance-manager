⚙️ Email Configuration (EmailJS)

To enable emailing of reports and digests, configure a free EmailJS service. Your settings will be stored in your private user document.

💡 How to set up EmailJS for free in 2 minutes: 1. Sign up for a free account at EmailJS.com.
2. Add a new email service (like Gmail) and copy the Service ID.
3. Create a new Email Template. In the design editor, place these fields:
    • {{to_email}} in the "To Email" field.
    • {{subject}} in the "Subject" field.
    • {{{message_html}}} (three curly braces) in the message body so HTML is rendered correctly.
    • Click "Attachment" and specify the path: {{attachment}}.
4. Copy the Template ID and save it above.
5. Go to the "API Keys" page, copy your Public Key, and save it above.
